Veritune

Unlock the symphony of your surroundings. An AR app design that takes external inputs and curates songs based on your surrounding.

Timeline- 4 weeks Role- UX/UI Designer Type- App Ideation,Research, Design, Prototype Team Members- Justin Charles (PM), Emma Crane, Alyse Balderrama

The Problem

Have you ever been stuck trying to find the perfect song? Whether you're hosting a party, going on a walk, or just doing chores it can be difficult to find the songs that match the mood. Through our research, we saw the potential of using AR technolgy to build a tool that users can unlock the music of their surrounds-- giving way to the development of Veritune.
